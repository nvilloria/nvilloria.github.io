Welcome!
My current research program has three foci: (i) understanding how US biofuels and trade policies affect the competitiveness of US agriculture in international markets; (ii) studying the vulnerabilities of the global food system to disruptions in critical maritime chokepoints, and (iii) investigating how market structure and non-competitive dynamics affect both the effectiveness of import restrictions in reducing soy-driven tropical deforestation and global soy markets.
